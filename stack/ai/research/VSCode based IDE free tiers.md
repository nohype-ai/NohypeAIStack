# AI IDE Free Tier Rotation Cheat Sheet (March 2026)  

## Cursor
- **Free usage**: 50 premium agent requests + 2,000 tab completions/month  
- **Refresh**: Monthly (exact date = your account creation/anniversary)  
- **Expensive models**: Frontier/premium (Claude Sonnet, GPT-4o, etc.) drain the 50 fastest  
- **Manage account**: https://cursor.com/dashboard  
- **Monitor usage**: https://cursor.com/dashboard (or IDE Settings → Usage tab)

## Antigravity (Google)
- **Free usage**: Meaningful weekly baseline quota (unlimited tabs + commands; exact tokens not published)  
- **Refresh**: Every 7 days (countdown shown in UI)  
- **Expensive**: Complex agent sessions, tool use, large context (Gemini 3 Pro & Claude Opus cost more than Flash)  
- **Manage account**: https://one.google.com/ai  
- **Monitor usage**: Settings → Models (per-model % + timer)

## Windsurf
- **Free usage**: 25 prompt credits/month (~100 GPT-4-class prompts; + unlimited Tab/Previews/Cascade Base)  
- **Refresh**: Monthly  
- **Expensive models**: Frontier (Claude/GPT) = 1 credit/prompt; lighter models = 0.25 credit  
- **Manage account**: https://windsurf.com (login → account)  
- **Monitor usage**: Settings (Cmd + ,) → Cascade → Plan **or** status bar → Settings → Plan

## Kiro
- **Free usage**: 50 credits/month  
- **Refresh**: Monthly (start of billing cycle)  
- **Expensive**: Complex agentic/multi-file tasks (credits scale with complexity)  
- **Manage account**: https://kiro.dev (in-app profile/upgrade)  
- **Monitor usage**: Bottom-right status bar ("Kiro Free Bonus X/500") → click for details
